There are several types of soil:

  • Sandy Soil – light, drains quickly, but low in nutrients.
  • Clay Soil – heavy, holds water, but can be hard for roots.
  • Loamy Soil – a balanced mix of sand, silt, and clay; best for farming.
  • Silty Soil – smooth, fertile, but can compact easily.
